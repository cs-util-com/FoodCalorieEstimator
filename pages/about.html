About CalorieCam

Gemini-powered calorie estimation that stays on your device.

Disclaimers

CalorieCam provides best-effort estimates powered by Gemini. Results can be off by a wide margin depending on image quality, portion size, and model limitations. Always use personal judgement and professional guidance for nutrition or medical decisions.
